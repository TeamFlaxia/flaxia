# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 10, 2026  
**Last Updated:** March 19, 2026 (Addition of advertisement delivery information)

## **Article 1 Information We Collect**

### **Account Information**
- Email address — Used for login only, not made public
- Username — Public information (cannot be changed after registration)
- Display name, bio, avatar image — Public information (can be changed)

### **Usage Information**
- Posts, hashtags, Fresh votes, follow information — Public information
- Session cookies — httpOnly, Secure, SameSite=Lax
- Server logs — IP address, User-Agent, timestamp (via Cloudflare infrastructure)

### **Analytics & Advertisement Information**
- **Cloudflare Web Analytics** — Privacy-first analytics tool that does not collect IP addresses. No cookies used.
- **Playable ad measurement** — Only aggregate counts are collected via the API; no user information is collected.

## **Article 2 Information We Do Not Collect**
- We do not perform our own ad tracking.
- We do not sell data or directly provide it to third parties.
- We do not collect payment information.

## **Article 3 Purpose of Information Use**
- Account authentication and management
- Service provision and improvement
- Detection of spam and fraudulent activity
- Advertisement delivery and performance measurement

## **Article 5 Data Retention Period**
- Account data is retained until account deletion.
- Posts are retained as `[deleted]` even after account deletion.
- Session data is deleted immediately upon logout.

## **Article 6 Cookies**
This service uses its own cookies (httpOnly, Secure, SameSite=Lax) for login session management.

## **Article 7 User Rights**

### **Rights under Japanese Personal Information Protection Law**
- Right to request disclosure of retained personal information
- Right to request correction, addition, or deletion of content
- Right to request suspension of use or erasure

### **Rights under GDPR (EU Users)**
- Right of access
- Right to rectification
- Right to erasure (right to be forgotten)
- Right to restriction of processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to object

To exercise these rights, please contact us via the in-app report button or at [RemydreS@proton.me](mailto:RemydreS@proton.me). After identity verification, we will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

## **Article 8 Cross-Border Data Transfer**
This service uses Cloudflare's global infrastructure, and data may be processed on servers outside Japan. EU user data is transferred with appropriate safeguards in place.

## **Article 9 Disclosure of Sender Information**
If there is a formal disclosure request based on laws (such as the Information Platform Countermeasure Law or the Provider Liability Limitation Law) or a court order, the operator may disclose the following sender information:

- IP address and timestamp
- Account information (email address, username)
- Post content and timestamp

## **Article 10 Minors' Privacy**
Account registration is not permitted for individuals under the age of 13. If we discover personal information from a user under 13, we will delete the account and associated data.

## **Article 11 Changes to Privacy Policy**
If this policy is changed, we will notify users on Flaxia at least 7 days before the effective date.

## **Article 12 Contact Information**
- Email: [teamflaxia@proton.me](mailto:teamflaxia@proton.me)
