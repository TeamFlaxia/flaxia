# About Flaxia

**Flaxia** is an innovative social networking service where your posts become playable experiences.

We believe social media should be more than just a feed of text, images, and videos. It should be interactive, immersive, and above all, fun.

## Our Mission
To build a platform where "creativity" and "interactivity" converge. We aim to create a world where users can not only share their thoughts and experiences but also distribute playable games, applications, and interactive content.

### Why Choose Flaxia
- **Rich Text**: Markdown and LaTeX support for text rendering. Go beyond plain text with beautifully formatted content.
- **Playable Posts**: Unlike traditional social media, Flaxia lets you embed fully functional games and applications directly into posts. Supports Flash, HTML5, WebAssembly (Wasm), and more.
- **Interactive Timeline**: As you scroll through your timeline, each post becomes a unique experience.
- **Developer-Friendly**: Built on modern web technologies to support a wide range of creative projects.
- **Community-Driven**: Designed for creators, developers, and innovators.

## Technology

Flaxia is built on the following modern web technologies:
- Cloudflare Workers for global performance — fast performance from anywhere in the world.
- Modern JavaScript/TypeScript for smooth and polished interactions.
- Interactive UI/UX design.

Flaxia currently supports files up to 10MB in the following formats:
- Images: GIF, PNG, JPG/JPEG
- Audio: MP3, WAV, OGG, M4A, WebM
- Interactive Content:
  - Flash (.swf)
  - ZIP archives containing index.html
    - HTML, JavaScript, CSS, WebAssembly (Wasm), and more. **Name the entry file index.html!**

For interactive content, you can attach a thumbnail (1MB or less) in:
- JPG/JPEG
- PNG
- GIF
